About Us

The E-Document System is an internal document management platform designed to enhance collaboration and streamline workflows within organizations. Inspired by Google Drive, our system ensures that all your documents are easily accessible, secure, and organized.

With roles ranging from Executives to Members, our system is tailored to meet diverse needs while maintaining robust security and control.
